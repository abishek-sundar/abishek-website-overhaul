About Me

Ever since my inception to internet and technology, I have always found joy in tinkering with any hardware or software that I manage to get my hands on. I understand that writing code that is easy to test is an acquired trait and work on perfecting my craft everyday. I enjoy anything to do with algorithms or back-end programming and love the sense of accomplishment in developing features across the full stack as well.

Apart from coding, I enjoy basketball, podcasts and video games. I keep up with what's going on in the finance world and am passionate about entrepreneurship. See my work under my projects!
